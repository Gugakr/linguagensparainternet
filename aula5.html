The ingredients
• 100 g.flour
• 10 g.sugar
• 1 cup water
• 2 eggs
• salt,pepper
The procedure
1. Mix dry ingredients thoroughly.
2. Pour in wet ingredients.
3. Mix for 10 minutes.
4. bake for one hour at 300 degrees.
Notes:
     The recipe may be improved by adding raisins.
Flour:
powder obtained by grinding certain cereals.
Sugar:
A sweet, liquid-soluble, industrially produced substance extracted especially from sugar cane and sugar beet.
Water:
A liquid substance (H2O) that is colorless, tasteless, and odorless, essential for the life of most living organisms and an excellent solvent for many other substances; hydrogen oxide.
Egg:
The egg of birds, especially chicken eggs.
Salt:
common name for sodium chloride (or table salt).
Pepper:
A spicy condiment, sialagogue, and rubefacient prepared with the fruits of these plants.
